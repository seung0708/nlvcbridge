COVID-19 UPDATE

Updated on March 13, 2020

The leaderships of both New Life Vision Church's English Ministry & Korean Ministry decided to have all our worships (only) ONLINE. During this time, we want to be wise but also continue to care for you. We also want you to continue to grow in your relationships with Christ.

Sunday Worship: Online Only


  • There will be no on-site service, so please join us online.

  • We will start at our normal time of 11:30AM and finish by 12:30PM.

  • Praise & Sermon will be broadcast live on Youtube.

  • You can click here for the live broadcast or you can go to www.nlvcbridge.com and click on "WATCH LIVE” on top of the site.

  • By any chance if you miss our worship time, the sermon video will be made available soon after 12:30PM.

  • During this time as we are physically separated, we want to make every effort to continue to connect as a body of Christ to encourage, strengthen, and pray for one another. That is the reason why we want to invite all of you to join us during this next week to be a part of one or more of the opportunities we are providing for you to be "together" with one another.

Bridge Groups: Online Only


  • Bridge groups will resume meeting online via Zoom/Google Hangout from 8:00PM - 9:00PM.

We are Available


  • Monday Night: Join us online (Zoom), the link will be posted on our Facebook group, from 8:00PM - 9:00PM. We will have a time of devotional, Q&A, and prayer.

  • Pastor Ben will be visiting different Bridge Groups throughout the week via online.

  • Friday Devotional: Every Friday night, we will post a short video devotional on our Facebook group.

  • If you need to reach us, we are available.
